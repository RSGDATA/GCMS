## ğŸ¨ Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary:** Deep blue (trust, professionalism)
- **Accent:** Gold/amber (elegance, classical music)
- **Background:** White or light gray (clean, modern)

### Fonts:
- **Headings:** Bold, sans-serif (Helvetica, Arial)
- **Body:** Clean, readable (Calibri, Open Sans)
- **Size:** Large enough to read from back of room

### Images:
- **High quality** (minimum 1920x1080)
- **Professional** (no clip art)
- **Relevant** (orchestra, music, data, success)
- **Consistent style** (all photos or all illustrations)

### Layout:
- **Minimal text** (6 words per bullet max)
- **Lots of white space**
- **One idea per slide**
- **Visual hierarchy** (big numbers, icons, images)

## ğŸ¤ Presentation Tips

### Opening (2 min):
- Start with the problem (relatable)
- Use a story or example
- Set up the "why we need this"

### Middle (10-12 min):
- Show the solution (visual)
- Explain benefits (not features)
- Use real examples
- Keep it simple

### Closing (3-5 min):
- Summarize impact
- Show what's next
- Clear call to action
- Open for questions

### Delivery:
- **Speak slowly** - let ideas sink in
- **Use pauses** - for emphasis
- **Tell stories** - more memorable than facts
- **Show enthusiasm** - it's contagious
- **Make eye contact** - build connection
- **Use gestures** - emphasize points
